 Enneagram

In the Enneagram system, "The Investigator" is a nickname that can refer to individuals with Enneagram Type Five. People with this type are independent thinkers who value their autonomy and desire to be self-sufficient. They have a strong need for understanding and knowledge, and they are often very analytical and detail-oriented. They are known for their ability to think deeply about complex subjects and are often highly imaginative and creative.

 Astrology

In astrology, "The Investigator" is often used as a nickname for the sign of Scorpio. Scorpio is a water sign and is symbolized by the scorpion. People born under the sign of Scorpio are known for their strong intuition and their ability to get to the heart of a matter. They have a natural curiosity and a desire to understand the world around them, and they are not afraid to delve into complex or difficult subjects. Scorpios are often described as being private and reserved, with a tendency to keep their thoughts and feelings to themselves.
